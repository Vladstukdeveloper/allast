About us

Allast is a platform for finding people who want to make their project a reality. Experts in the team provide knowledge, help with the development of a plan and the search for financing. The participants prove why they should get into the top 15 startups and undergo the appropriate training. Participants get the opportunity to go through an intensive training process, during which they master key skills in business management, marketing, strategic planning and more

advantages

Participating in Alast can bring many benefits. First, it is an opportunity to receive financial support and investment in exchange for a share in the business. Second, participants gain valuable connections and mentoring support from experienced entrepreneurs and experts.

requirements

Allast accelerator participants go through several stages of development, including idea analysis, product development, validation, scaling, and market launch. The main requirements are the ability to communicate. Interesting ideas. Ability to think critically. Openness to change. Desire to learn.
